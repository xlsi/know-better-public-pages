Know Better Support

Help and support information for Know Better.

Contact Support

Email support@getknowbetter.app for help with Know Better.

What To Include

When emailing support, include these details with your request:

  • The email address used for Know Better sign-in.
  • Device model and iOS version.
  • A short description of what happened.
  • Screenshots if they help explain the issue.

Common Topics

  • Sign-in or email code problems.
  • Account deletion questions.
  • Sync, reminder, or lesson history issues.
  • Privacy Policy questions.
